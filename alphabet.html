Chapter : Alphabet Signs (ASL)

As you learned in the previous chapter, we will move step by step through different levels to learn sign language. In this first level, we will begin by understanding the alphabet signs used in American Sign Language (ASL).

Alphabet signs are the foundation of ASL communication. They are mainly used for spelling names, places, or words that do not have a specific sign. Learning these signs helps beginners understand how hand shapes represent letters.

Each letter in ASL is represented by a unique hand shape. By practicing these alphabet signs regularly, learners can improve their visual recognition and communication skills.
