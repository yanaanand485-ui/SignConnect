After learning alphabets and numbers, greetings are the next important step in sign language communication. Greetings help us start conversations politely and express respect and friendliness. In this chapter, you will learn some basic greeting signs that are commonly used in daily interactions. Practising these signs regularly will help you become more confident while communicating with others using sign language. In future chapters, we will continue learning more useful expressions and conversational signs.

As you continue your journey of learning sign language, it is completely natural to experience confusion or small challenges while understanding different hand movements and expressions. In the beginning, many learners feel that remembering signs or coordinating hand shapes takes more time than expected.

In this chapter, we are focusing on basic greetings that are used in everyday communication. Greetings are an important part of social interaction because they help us start conversations politely and build positive connections with others.

Sometimes you may feel that progress is slow or that you are making mistakes while practising. However, these moments are part of learning and improvement. With consistency and dedication, the same signs that feel difficult today will become easy and natural in the future.

At this stage, your goal is not to achieve perfection but to develop familiarity and confidence. In the upcoming chapters, you will explore more useful expressions and practical situations where sign language can be applied. Stay motivated, trust your learning process, and continue practising with curiosity and enthusiasm.
