In this chapter, we are beginning our journey of learning number signs in sign language. For now, we will focus only on counting from 0 to 10. These basic number signs are very important because they are used in daily communication such as telling age, time, quantity, and simple counting. Learning numbers step by step helps beginners build confidence and improve hand coordination. Once you become comfortable with these signs, we will continue learning higher numbers and more advanced counting patterns in future chapters. Keep observing the hand shapes carefully and try to practise them regularly to improve your recognition and signing skills.

In the previous section, you learned the basic number signs from 0 to 10. Now, in this video, you will be introduced to number signs from 10 to 30. These signs may appear slightly more detailed, but by watching the movements carefully, you will start recognizing patterns in larger numbers. At this stage, your goal is not to memorize every sign instantly. Instead, try to understand how counting progresses and how hand shapes change with increasing numbers. In future chapters, we will continue learning higher numbers and practical uses of counting in real-life sign language communication.
